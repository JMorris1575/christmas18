Welcome...

Welcome to the Family Christmas website for 2018. I've tried to make it friendlier to those who access the internet on tiny little cell-phone screens but I'm still just learning. Hard telling what I might have broken in the process.

If you see her name here and are wondering who Anne is, she is a friend of Scott's who is joining us this year. Welcome Anne!

As usual, you are to decipher my obvious clues and select whichever gift you want. Random Christmas memories will appear on every page and this year you can see them all if you click on "Memories" in the menu. You can add new ones too and I hope you will. It's a nice way for us to get to know one another all over again. Finally, check out the "Activities" entry in the menu from time to time. I've got some trivia questions up already and hope to add some other activities later.

The little black thing in all of the gift pictures is a camera lens cap. It is 2 1/4" across and is there to give you an idea of package sizes.
