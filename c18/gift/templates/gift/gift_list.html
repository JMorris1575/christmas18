Welcome...

Welcome to the 2018 version of the family Christmas website. I've tried to make it more friendly to those who access the internet on those tiny little cell-phone screens but I'm still just learning. Who knows what I might have broken in the process?

If you see her name here and are wondering who Anne is, she is a friend of Scott's who is joining us this year. Welcome Anne!

As usual, the idea is to decipher my obvious clues and select whichever gift you want. Also you can read random Christmas memories people have submitted or see them all if you click on "Memories" in the menu. You can add new ones too and I hope you will. It's a nice way to see a different side of people. Finally, check out the "Activities" entry in the menu from time to time. I've got some trivia questions set up already and hope to add some other activities later.

By the way, the little black round thing in all of the gift pictures is the lens cap from my camera. It is 2 1/4" in diameter and is there to give you an idea of the size of the packages.
